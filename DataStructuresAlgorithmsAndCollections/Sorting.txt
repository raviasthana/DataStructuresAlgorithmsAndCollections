Sorting
=======
Sorting is nothing but storage of the data in sorted order, it can be in ascending or descending order.

Sorting Efficiency
==================
There are many techniques for sorting. Implementation of a particular sorting technique depends upon 
situation. Sorting techniques mainly depend on two parameters. First is the execution time (time taken) 
and second is space (memory)

Types of Sorting Techniques
===========================
1) Bubble Sort
2) Insertion Sort
3) Selection Sort
4) Quick Sort
5) Merge Sort
6) Heap Sort
7) Time Sort
